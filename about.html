About Us

Welcome to Dawn Badminton Club, located in the heart of Erumely, Kerala. We are a community of passionate badminton players dedicated to promoting and enjoying the sport.

Our History

The Dawn Badminton Club was founded by Hari Kumar P R in 2010. Hari's vision was to create a space where badminton enthusiasts of all ages and skill levels could come together to play, learn, and grow. Over the years, the club has grown into a thriving community, known for its friendly atmosphere and top-notch facilities.

About Our Founder

Hari Kumar P R, a passionate badminton player and coach, established the Dawn Badminton Club with the dream of making badminton accessible to everyone in Erumely. Hari has dedicated his life to promoting the sport and has trained numerous players who have gone on to achieve great success at various levels of competition.

Why Badminton?

Badminton is a sport that offers numerous physical and mental benefits. It's a great way to stay fit, improve hand-eye coordination, and develop strategic thinking. At Dawn Badminton Club, we believe in the power of sport to bring people together, foster a sense of community, and promote a healthy lifestyle.

Our Facilities

We offer 24/7 court availability, private lessons, and regular tournaments to keep our members engaged and challenged. Our state-of-the-art facilities include well-maintained courts, and a comfortable lounge area for players to relax and socialize.

Join Us

Whether you're a seasoned player or just starting out, you'll find a welcoming community and plenty of opportunities to improve your game at Dawn Badminton Club. Come join us and experience the joy of badminton!

Join Dawn Badminton Club today for the best badminton experience in Erumely, Kerala.
